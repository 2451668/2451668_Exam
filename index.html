Exam Submission

This is my final website and exam submission for the semester, in which I showcase a simple site containing 5 main sections; this Homepage, a Portfolio, an Essay, a Blog, and a Design section.

About Me

This is the profile and portfolio portion of the site. I'll talk a little about myself here.

My name is Thabang Maleka, and I'm a 20-year-old, third year student at the University of the Witwatersrand. I'm currently enrolled in the WSOA Digital Art and Game Design programme, and have Interactive Media, Animation, and Digital Art Theory as my primary subjects.

Documentation

The goal for the website wasto showcases a bit about myself as well as illustrate some of the techincal skills covered during the semester. The primary audience would the marker, and as such most of the content has to do with fulfilling the requirements set in the rubric. I aimed to create a visually appealing and creative experience by use of interesting colours and abstract patterns, juxatoposed against simple white backgrounds in some sections, as well as a generally adherence to standard visual desigin principles.
